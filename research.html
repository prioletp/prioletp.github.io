Modelling Exozodiacal Dust

Using high-contrast interferometric data from VLTI/MATISSE, I model the infrared excess produced by exozodiacal dust close to main-sequence stars. The models explore disk morphology, grain composition, and temperature distribution.

Bayesian Inference and Model Selection

I employ nested sampling algorithms to estimate posterior distributions and compute Bayesian evidence, enabling robust model comparison even in multimodal parameter spaces.

Covariance-Aware Analysis of Interferometric Data

My recent work focuses on incorporating covariance matrices into visibility and closure phase likelihoods, improving accuracy in high-SNR regimes.
